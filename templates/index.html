About

Extracting and storing data from images made easy

1. Login

2. Choose an Image

3. Verify

4. Upload

In our day to day life, we meet many people, talk to many strangers and somehow manage to get their contact, mostly in the form of their visiting cards. But if you observe clearly, these cards won't be available at the time of need. There should be a safe box in order to save these details, so that it can be used whenever needed.

Smart ID Scanner project is a solution to this problem. This project is an approach to extract contact details of a person through an image, mostly from their Visiting Cards and save it to the database. You can access these details Anytime, from Anywhere!!!

All you need to do is, take the image of the visiting card or anything that contains contact details, and upload it to Smart ID Scanner Website. The website will automatically extract details from the image. You can also choose to make any changes,before uploading the extracted data to the Database.

Poratble

All your data, along with the visiting card images are stored in the database, which can be accessed from anywhere and at any point of time.

Simple UI

Our User-Interface is clean and simple. You don't need to worry about anything. All you need to do is Sign In, and start making use of it.

Safety

We don't compromise with data. Your data is safe with us. Only you can have control on your data and no one else.
